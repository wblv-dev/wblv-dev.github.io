ATS-CVC

CV gap analysis.
Not a CV writer.

This tool applies the ATS-CVC Framework to your CV against a specific job description. It identifies what you're missing, what's undersold, and what a hiring manager will probe on. It does not write your CV. That's your job.

🔒
Your data never leaves your browser. This tool has no backend, no server, and no database. Your CV, job description, and API key travel directly from your browser to your chosen AI provider — and nowhere else. This page makes one outbound connection only: to the API endpoint of whichever provider you select. I make no money from this tool. I see no data. Ever. Verify this in the source code →
How it works
01 —
Choose your AI provider. Mistral is genuinely free — phone number only, no card. The others require a minimum spend paid directly to that company.
02 —
Get your API key from your chosen provider and paste it below. Your key goes directly to them — not to this tool, not to me.
03 —
Upload your CV as a PDF and paste the job description. Confirm your name and the role title are correct.
04 —
Review your five-section gap analysis. Save as PDF. Close the tab — everything is gone. Nothing was ever stored.
